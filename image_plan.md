# Image Generation Plan for Blog Posts

## 1. 5 přírodních zabijáků erekce
**Thumbnail:** A dramatic composition showing unhealthy foods (greasy burger, sugary donut, alcohol) in dark, moody lighting with a "danger" or "warning" aesthetic. High contrast, professional food photography style.
**Content Image:** A close-up of a stressed man looking at a plate of unhealthy food, or a conceptual image of "clogged arteries" represented artistically (not gross). Let's go with: A healthy vs unhealthy meal comparison split screen, bright and clean lighting.

## 2. Testosteron po třicítce
**Thumbnail:** A fit, older man (35-45) jogging or lifting weights outdoors in golden hour sunlight. Radiating energy and vitality.
**Content Image:** A conceptual chart or graph overlay on a background of a man looking in the mirror, symbolizing self-reflection and health monitoring. Or: A selection of testosterone-boosting foods (eggs, nuts, broccoli) arranged aesthetically on a wooden table.

## 3. Kotvičník zemní (Tribulus)
**Thumbnail:** A high-quality macro shot of the Tribulus Terrestris plant (yellow flower and thorny fruit) in a natural setting with soft bokeh.
**Content Image:** Dried Tribulus herb in a wooden bowl or as a tea preparation, suggesting natural remedy and tradition.

## 4. Erektilní dysfunkce vs. psychika
**Thumbnail:** A double exposure or artistic composition showing a man's silhouette with "stormy" clouds or tangled lines in the head area, representing mental turmoil.
**Content Image:** A man sitting peacefully in a meditation pose or consulting with a doctor/therapist (friendly, professional atmosphere), symbolizing the solution.

## 5. TOP 7 potravin pro pevnou erekci
**Thumbnail:** A vibrant, colorful flat lay of "erection superfoods": watermelon, dark chocolate, nuts, oysters, pomegranate. Fresh and appetizing.
**Content Image:** A close-up of a hand preparing a healthy smoothie or salad with these ingredients.

## 6. Jak stres v práci zabíjí váš sexlife
**Thumbnail:** A tired businessman in a suit loosening his tie, looking exhausted in a dim office late at night. Blue/grey color palette.
**Content Image:** A couple in bed, back to back, looking distant, symbolizing the impact of stress on relationships.

## 7. Kegelovy cviky pro muže
**Thumbnail:** An anatomical 3D render style or abstract vector art showing the pelvic floor muscles (tasteful, medical/educational style). Or: A man doing a plank or yoga pose that engages the core. Let's go with the fit man doing core exercises.

## 8. Afrodiziaka, která skutečně fungují
**Thumbnail:** Elegant composition of oysters, chili peppers, and chocolate on a dark slate background. Luxury/sensual vibe.

## 9. Ranní erekce: Co vám říká
